Welcome to Feldor Technology



About Us

Feldor Technology is a dynamic start-up based in Gwarinpa, Nigeria. Our mission is to revolutionize the E-commerce sector and empower individuals with cutting-edge technology skills.


What We Offer

At Feldor Technology, we provide top-notch training in various aspects of technology. Our courses cover a wide range of topics, including Python programming, Java programming, UI and UX design, Data Analysis, and much more. Our goal is to bridge the gap between aspiration and expertise, helping learners from all backgrounds realize their potential in the tech world.


Our Vision

We envision a future where technology is accessible to all, propelling innovation and driving positive change in society. With a strong emphasis on E-commerce development and technical training, we strive to create a well-rounded learning ecosystem that nurtures talent and fosters excellence.


Why Choose Feldor Technology?

With experienced instructors, practical hands-on projects, and a commitment to excellence, Feldor Technology stands as a premier platform for aspiring tech enthusiasts. We pride ourselves on providing a supportive and engaging learning environment, encouraging every individual to reach their fullest potential in the tech industry.
