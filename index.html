About Us

It is with great pleasure and delight that I welcome you to Laylah’s Way, an initiative on the mission to empower underprivileged boys and girls through relevant education, skills development, innovative healthcare, and focused livelihood programs. Read more


Mission

To provide people affected by human and natural crises in the Horn of Nigeria with emergency relief, educational programs, clean water and food security, health care, protection for children and orphans, sustainable socio-economic programs, and to promote peace-building.


Our Vision

We envision a day when there is no more poverty in the Horn of Nigeria and every person’s dignity is protected, especially those affected by human and natural crises.
